## The Vision

Millions of students and working professionals live away from home.

While food delivery has become easier than ever, finding authentic home-cooked meals remains difficult.

MelitX was created to bridge that gap.

Our mission is simple:

> Make quality home-cooked food accessible, discoverable, and scalable.

MelitX enables home chefs to build sustainable businesses while helping customers discover trusted meals prepared in real kitchens nearby.

## What MelitX Offers

### 👨‍🍳 For Home Chefs

* Kitchen onboarding & verification
* Daily meal publishing
* Weekly menus
* Subscription plans
* Meal combos & add-ons
* Order management
* Reviews & ratings
* Earnings visibility

### 🍽️ For Customers

* Discover nearby home kitchens
* One-time meal ordering
* Weekly & monthly tiffin subscriptions
* Real-time order tracking
* Ratings & reviews
* Personalized meal experiences

### 🚚 For Delivery Partners

* Delivery management
* Pickup & drop workflows
* Real-time delivery updates
* Integrated order lifecycle

## Founder Note

MelitX started with a simple observation:

People don't just miss food when they move away from home.

They miss familiarity.

They miss comfort.

They miss the feeling attached to a home-cooked meal.

MelitX is our attempt to use technology to reconnect people with that experience while creating meaningful opportunities for home chefs.
